Welcome to Student Organizations Hub

Explore student-led organizations in Computer & IT, Ecom, Engineering, Medicine, and Health Sciences. These groups offer excellent platforms to connect, lead, and grow. Enhance your academic journey while building lifelong connections.

JUSA – University Student Association

JUSA nurtures leadership and engagement through faculty-specific structures and annual elections.

JESA – Engineering Students

JESA represents engineering students, advocating their needs and facilitating faculty decisions.

JEMSA – Ecom & Management

Developing future leaders in management by empowering students with decision-making skills.

JUMSA – Medical Students

Supporting academic growth and personal development for health science students.

JUTSA – IT Students

An inclusive tech community helping students grow technically and professionally.
