Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions and answers to help you get started.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions portal or visit the campus for assistance.

Jamhuriya University is located on Digfeer Street, Hodan District, Mogadishu.

We offer faculties including Computer & IT, Medicine, Engineering, and Management.
